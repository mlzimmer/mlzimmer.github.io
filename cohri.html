CoHRI

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)'s Collaborative Human-Robot Interaction (CoHRI) dataset will contain three distinct subsets, with the potential for future expansion based on the needs of the community. We envision these subsets as increasing in complexity, though each is an essential part of understanding human motion and intention. As the target audience is researchers in the HRI domain, we hope that providing a useful basis for collaborative interaction-based robot learning will encourage HRI studies that are more reproducible. Additionally, though this does not directly affect the standards community, the use of a common dataset may lead to more comparable studies, benefiting the research community and enabling future standards efforts.
